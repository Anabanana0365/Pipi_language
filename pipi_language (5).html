Pipi Language

🤫 About Pipi Language

Pipi Language is a fun secret cipher that transforms your text into a mysterious code!

How It Works:

  • Adds "pi" before every vowel (a, e, i, o, u)
  • Adds "po" before every consonant
  • Leaves spaces, punctuation, letters from other languages unchanged

Example:

Normal: "Hello World"
Pipi: "poHpielpoloplopoo poWpioporporpold"

Button Functions:

  • 🔒 Encode to Pipi: Converts normal text to Pipi Language (Press "E")
  • 🔓 Decode from Pipi: Converts Pipi Language back to normal text (Press "D")
  • 🔄 Reverse Translation: Flips whatever is in the result field (Press "R")
  • 📋 Copy Result: Copies the translated text to your clipboard (Press "C")

Perfect for sending secret messages to friends who know the code or have this file!
